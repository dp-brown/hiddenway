Not sure which you have?

Check a payslip. If your pension line reduces your gross salary, it's salary sacrifice. If it's deducted before tax is calculated, it's from pay before tax. If you transfer money in yourself, your provider adds basic rate tax relief and you claim the rest through your tax return.

Contributions to a personal pension get tax relief added automatically.
